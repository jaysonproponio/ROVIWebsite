Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our products, select your desired items, choose your size (if applicable), and add them to your cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout and fill in your shipping and payment information. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is placed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment methods. All transactions are processed securely to protect your information.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery (2-3 business days). Shipping times may vary based on your location and the shipping method selected.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with tags attached. Please visit our Returns page for detailed information on how to return items.

How do I know what size to order?

We provide detailed size charts for all our products. Please refer to our Size Guide for accurate measurements. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to select international destinations. Please check our shipping information at checkout to see if we deliver to your country. International shipping times and costs may vary.

How do I add items to my wishlist?

To add items to your wishlist, simply click the bookmark icon on any product page. You can view all your saved items by clicking the heart icon in the header navigation. Items in your wishlist can be easily added to your cart later.

What if my item is out of stock?

If an item is out of stock, you can add it to your wishlist to be notified when it becomes available again. We regularly restock popular items, so check back often!

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team via email at support@rovi.com or by phone at +1 (555) 123-4567. Our team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. You can also visit our Contact page for more information.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! We offer gift wrapping services for an additional fee. You can select this option during checkout. We also include a personalized gift message with your order.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely! All our products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized designers and retailers. We guarantee the authenticity of every item we sell.
