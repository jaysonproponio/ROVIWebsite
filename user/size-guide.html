How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape measure level.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your waist, usually just above the belly button.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the tape measure level.
  • Shoe Size: Measure your foot length from heel to toe while standing. Use the longest measurement.

Fit Notes

  • Our sizes may vary slightly by style and designer
  • If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit
  • Some items may have a relaxed or fitted cut - check individual product descriptions
  • For questions about specific items, please contact our customer service team
